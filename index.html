We budget money. We track meals and workouts. But time — the only resource you can't get back — mostly slips by unexamined. DeepTime treats your hours like they're worth something. Because they are.

Three tenets

Everything in the app serves one of these.

01

Set intention

Every time you open your Mac, DeepTime asks one question: what deserves your focus? Pick a project — or honestly claim FreeTime — before the scroll picks for you.

02

Stay focused

A quiet, project-colored timer lives at the edge of your screen the whole session. Not a nag — a presence. You always know what you committed to.

03

Understand your time

Daily and weekly goals, pace bars, a monthly heat map, streaks, and all-time totals per project. Your real hours, honestly counted — sleep and idle never inflate the numbers.
